One of the Most important aspects in investing are the P/E Ratio and Market Cap

Lets talk about them briefly before we proceed to the predictions

The price-earnings ratio (P/E Ratio) is the ratio for valuing a company that measures its current share price relative to its per-share earnings. For example, suppose that a company is currently trading at $43 a share and its earnings over the last 12 months were $1.95 per share. Find out more.

Market cap -- or market capitalization -- refers to the total value of all a company's shares of stock. It is calculated by multiplying the price of a stock by its total number of outstanding shares. For example, a company with 20 million shares selling at $50 a share would have a market cap of $1 billion. Find out more.
